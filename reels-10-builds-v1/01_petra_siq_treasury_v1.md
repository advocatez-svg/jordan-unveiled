# Reel 01 — Petra Destination Mini-Reel

**Account:** Jordan Unveiled · Travel & Tourism
**Format:** 9:16 vertical · 1080×1920 · 30 fps · 15s · 4 clips
**Audience:** Cultural short-haul travellers 25–45, Gulf + Europe
**Tone:** Aspirational, warm, confident — premium but inviting
**Goal:** Stop the scroll in 1s, build desire, maximize saves + shares

---

## 1. Hook (1–2s)

Open in the narrow Siq, walls pressing in, a sliver of the Treasury's rose
façade just edging into the gap ahead.

| # | Hook line | Read |
|---|-----------|------|
| A | **The moment Petra appears.** | Curiosity + payoff promise. Names the exact emotional beat the clip delivers. |
| B | 2,000 years old. Still breathtaking. | Strong, but stat-led — slower to land emotionally. |
| C | Walk into the Siq with me. | Warm POV, but soft; weaker scroll-stopper. |

**Recommended: A — "The moment Petra appears."**
It sets an open loop ("the moment") that the Treasury reveal pays off two
seconds later. The viewer stays to see *the moment* — exactly the dwell-time
behaviour the algorithm rewards. It also works for cold audiences who don't
yet know Petra by name.

## 3. Kinetic Typography

- **Headline:** Anton (or Bebas Neue) — uppercase, tight tracking.
- **Support:** Montserrat Medium/SemiBold — italic for location tags.
- **Animation:** vertical mask-reveal — each line wipes up from a hidden
  baseline, synced to the track's kick. Clip 2 headline ("PETRA") snaps in on
  the audio swell.
- Max **5 words per line**, max 2 lines on screen.
- Drop shadow + 60–70% scrim behind text on busy frames for contrast.

---

## 4. Color & Grade

- **Palette:** warm sandstone rose, golden key light, deep shadow contrast.
- LUT direction: lift warmth in mids, push rose into the stone highlights,
  protect shadow detail in the Siq (don't crush to black — keep texture).
- Whites slightly warm; no cool/teal contamination.
- Apply **one grade across all 4 clips** so the Siq, Treasury, valley and
  Monastery read as a single film.

---

## 5. Motion & Pacing

- Elegant, deliberate cuts — this is a "reveal" reel, not a fast cutdown.
- Smooth ease-in/ease-out on every camera move.
- **Signature move:** slow continuous push-in on the Treasury reveal (Clip 2),
  timed so the closest framing lands on the audio swell.
- Cut on footstep/beat in Clip 1 to create rhythm before the reveal.

---

## 6. Audio

Building cinematic track; swell **lands exactly on the Treasury reveal at ~3.5s**
(start of Clip 2), then sustains and lifts again into the Monastery.

Track-style references:
1. **Hans Zimmer / "Time"-style** slow-build piano + strings swell.
2. **Epic Middle-Eastern cinematic** — duduk/oud lead over rising percussion.
3. **Trending IG "epic reveal" stems** — soft pad intro → boom + riser hit on
   the drop (search "cinematic reveal swell" in the in-app library).

> Use a licensed/trending audio in-app for reach; keep the swell aligned to 3.5s.

## How to Build This

**Best tool:** CapCut (mobile or desktop) for the edit + beat-sync; Premiere/
After Effects if you want frame-accurate mask-reveals and the push-in.

**The single most important thing to nail:** the **Treasury reveal at ~3.5s
landing on the audio swell**. Everything else supports that one beat — if the
visual reveal and the musical swell hit on the same frame, the reel works; if
they drift apart, it doesn't.

**Footage to source:** all four clips need real Petra footage — Siq walking POV,
Treasury reveal (shoot it as one continuous walk-out if possible), valley wide
(drone or high vantage), and the Monastery. Flag: drone use in Petra needs a
permit — source ground-based wide if no aerial available.
