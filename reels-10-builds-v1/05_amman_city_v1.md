## 3. Kinetic Typography

- **Headline:** modern condensed (Anton / Bebas Neue), uppercase.
- **Support:** clean sans (Montserrat Medium), italic for tags.
- **Animation:** slide-in reveals — text enters from the side and settles on
  the beat; support fades up beneath.
- Max **4–5 words per line.**
- High-contrast white type; on the bright white-hills frames add a soft scrim
  or use a warm-charcoal text color for legibility against pale stone.

---

## 4. Color & Grade

- **Palette:** warm beige city tones, sunset gold, soft contrast.
- Keep the white stone clean and warm (not blown out); lift shadows gently for
  a soft, premium city look.
- Dusk clip warms into amber as lights turn on.
- One grade across all six clips for cohesion.

## 5. Motion & Pacing

- Energetic but smooth — quicker cuts than the nature reels, still eased.

  inject urban energy, landing on the track's drop.
- Vary the rhythm: slow on the cityscape/Citadel, fast on the street, settle on
  the rooftop.

## How to Build This

**Best tool:** CapCut for the multi-clip beat-sync + speed-ramp; it handles a
6-clip, 20s cutdown fast and the auto-beat markers help time the drop.

**The single most important thing to nail:** **cohesion across six clips so
Amman reads as one premium city, not a stock montage.** One grade, one type
system, one rising track — and the speed-ramp on Rainbow Street as the single
energy spike. If the clips feel mismatched, the "underrated capital" promise
collapses.

**Footage to source:** white-hills cityscape (drone), Citadel columns, Roman
Theatre, Rainbow Street walking footage (shoot at 60fps for the ramp), a
rooftop café POV, and a dusk city-lights shot. Flag: the rooftop-with-view and
the dusk lights are the two hardest to capture well — scout a rooftop café with
a clean western view for golden hour.
