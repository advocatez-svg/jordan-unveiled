## 1. Hook (1–2s)

Open on a fast montage tease — 3 rapid flashes (Petra, Wadi Rum, Dead Sea) in
under 2 seconds.

| # | Hook line | Read |
|---|-----------|------|
| A | **The perfect 3 days in Jordan.** | Confident, complete-plan promise; "perfect" + a number = save-bait. |
| B | Save this exact itinerary. | Action-led, but pre-empts the CTA at the top. |
| C | Petra, Wadi Rum, Dead Sea — in 3 days. | Names the payoff up front; great, but reads as a list not a promise. |

**Recommended: A — "The perfect 3 days in Jordan."**
"Perfect" + a fixed number signals a tidy, authoritative plan worth bookmarking,
and it sets up the chapter structure that follows. It outperforms the
CTA-as-hook (B) by keeping the save ask for the end, and beats the list (C) by
promising a *plan* rather than just naming places.

## 3. Kinetic Typography

- **Chapter labels:** bold condensed (Anton / Bebas Neue), uppercase — large
  "DAY 1 / DAY 2 / DAY 3" with the location beneath.
- **Location captions:** clean sans (Montserrat Medium), italic, smaller.
- **Animation:** **mask-reveal transitions between days** — the day label wipes
  in as the new chapter opens; location captions fade up.
- Max **3 words per line.**
- High-contrast white type with shadow/scrim; **hold each DAY label on screen
  ≥2.5s** so it's readable and the structure is unmistakable.

---

## 4. Color & Grade

- **One consistent cinematic warm grade across all three chapters** — Petra
  rose-sandstone, Wadi Rum rust-red, Dead Sea turquoise-gold all pulled toward a
  shared warm spine so the reel feels like one film.
- Lifted blacks, warm highlights; let each location keep its signature hue within
  the unified grade.

---

## 5. Motion & Pacing

- **Chapter beats** — energy steps up at each new day; a stronger transition

- **Speed-ramp into Day 2 (Wadi Rum, ~9.5s)** — the kinetic peak of the reel,
  landing on the track's biggest energy step.
- Within each day: shot 1 establishes, shot 2 pays off; smooth eased moves.

## How to Build This

**Best tool:** CapCut for the chapter-based cutdown, mask-reveal transitions,
speed-ramp, and beat-sync across 7 clips in 30s; it makes the DAY-label
animations and audio markers fast.

**The single most important thing to nail:** **clear, legible DAY 1 / DAY 2 /
DAY 3 chapter labels that hold long enough to read.** This is a *save* reel — its
value is the structure. If the chapter labels are obvious and on-screen ≥2.5s,
viewers grasp "this is a usable plan" and bookmark it. Rushed or unclear labels
turn a save-worthy itinerary into just another montage.

**Footage to source:** 2 clips each for Petra, Wadi Rum and Dead Sea — reuse the
best shots from Reels 01–03 (Siq/Treasury, Monastery, dunes aerial, jeep/camp,
float, mud/sunset). Flag: shoot the Wadi Rum aerial at 60fps so the Day-2
speed-ramp works; ensure all reused clips share the unified warm grade.
