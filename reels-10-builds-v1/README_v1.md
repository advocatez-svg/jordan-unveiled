# Jordan Unveiled — Reel Builds v1

Shot-by-shot creative build sheets for 10 Instagram Reels / TikTok videos
promoting Jordan travel & tourism.

**Standard spec for every reel:** 9:16 vertical · 1080×1920 · 30 fps.
**UI-safe zone:** keep all text clear of the bottom 250px and top 150px.
**Text rule:** under 6 words per line, high contrast, one idea per clip,
no clichés or default transitions.

Each build sheet includes: recommended hook (with rationale), a scene/clip
breakdown table, kinetic typography, color & grade, motion & pacing, audio
references with the key beat marked, CTA + end frame, caption + hashtags, an
A/B variant, and a "How to build this" note (best tool + the one thing to nail).

## Notes

- Audio references describe **track styles** — source the actual track from the
  platform's licensed/trending in-app library for reach and rights.
- Footage that must be sourced or shot fresh is flagged per reel in the
  "How to build this" note.
- The Arabic phrases reel (08) follows a strict bilingual rule: Arabic, English,
  and pronunciation each on their own separate line — never mixed. Have a native
  speaker verify before publishing.
